About KLASSICAL Delicacies

KLASSICAL Delicacies, founded by Bukola Oyeniyi, brings 15 years of expertise in curating exceptional dishes that blend traditional Nigerian flavors with continental cuisine. Her passion for creating unique, relish-worthy meals inspired the name KLASSICAL Delicacies, reflecting her commitment to classic culinary artistry.

From intimate gatherings to grand events, Bukola and her team deliver nationwide, ensuring every occasion is elevated with unforgettable flavors and impeccable service.
